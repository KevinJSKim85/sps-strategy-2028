## 10. Essay Narrative Strategy

The strongest essay narrative for this student is neither "I am good at math" (thousands write this) nor "I love Latin" (others write this too). The key is the intersection of both:

"I am drawn to systems of precise and beautiful rules -- whether that system is the grammar of the dead languages that built Western civilization, or the abstract structures of modern mathematics. Both demand the same kind of mind."

This is a rare, logical, and memorable intellectual identity. Skiing adds a third dimension: physical discipline, international competition, and persistence.

This narrative provides an answer to "Why you?" that scores and awards alone can never create.

Please review this document together with the SPS College Advising Office (Greg Buckles, Erin Ainor). TOEFL policy may change annually, so re-check each university website before submitting applications.
